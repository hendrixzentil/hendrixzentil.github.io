About.

⠀⠀⠀⠀Since starting my fitness journey with Taekwondo at age 12 and learning about the venerable lineage of discipline and principles that underpin traditional martial arts, I have cultivated a deep respect for mental resilience, physical conditioning, and the pursuit of personal excellence.

This path has led me to question what purpose "personal excellence" serves, and my open-ended answer to that is currently "to do good."

To find out what "doing good" was, I looked to history to discern how others have contributed to their societies, overcome obstacles, and impact those around them.

My studies have led me to finance and economics, fields that inspire me with their potential to drive meaningful change. Now, I dedicate myself to mastering these fields, and strive to uplift others, create opportunities, and contribute to a sustainable, meaningful future.
